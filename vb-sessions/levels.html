Levels

Our levels exist to keep every session safe and enjoyable for everyone on court. Players are expected to be honest about their ability and to bring good sportsmanship regardless of level. If you sign up for a session above your level you may be asked to leave without a refund.

Pre-approval required for Advanced and Competitive sessions. Attend a Rep or Clinic session to get rated before booking.
Beginner Recreational

New to volleyball or still building the basics. You're learning the rules, technique, and rotations. These sessions focus on development and fun — not intensity.

ServingCan get a serve in from the baseline most of the time (any style)
PassingCan direct a free ball toward the setter zone
SettingCan bump or overhand set a good pass to an attacker
AttackingUnderstands the approach; attack is not a safety hazard
RotationsAware of social rotation; learning 5-1 / 6-2 concepts
Intermediate LVA Division 3

You play regularly and are comfortable on court. You understand rotations and can execute the core skills of your position with reasonable consistency. May not have played on an organised team, but you know the game.

ServingOverhand from baseline, in 8/10 times
Passing7/10 passes in the setter zone; rarely aces
SettingCan hand-set outside and opposite on good passes
AttackingConsistent 3–4 step approach; ball in 8/10 attempts
RotationsUnderstands 5-1 / 6-2 and transitions; correct position 8/10
Advanced LVA Divisions 1–2 Pre-approval required

Played on an organised team with coaching. Strong fundamentals, some advanced skills (jump serves, combinations), and a clear positional role. You read the game and can adapt when things go wrong.

ServingConsistent jump float or topspin; targets opponents' weaknesses
Passing5/10 in-system passes regardless of serve type
SettingRuns an offense with a game plan; can set tempo balls and fix bad passes
Attacking25 %+ efficiency; multiple swing types and locations
BlockingEffective line and cross blocks; solid 1v1 capability
Defense30 %+ dig rate; understands positioning shifts
Competitive LVA Première · Superleague Pre-approval required

Elite-level play. College, club, semi-professional, or international experience. Exceptional volleyball IQ, physical ability, and the ability to perform consistently under pressure for the full session.

Serving9/10 float, 7/10 topspin to intended location; advanced techniques
Passing60 %+ in-system; commands reception; takes more than a third of the court
SettingAdapts game plan in real time; runs all combinations; fixes bad passes consistently
Attacking30 %+ efficiency; hits all court locations with power and accuracy
Blocking1+ block/set; line, seam, cross, hard cross and split block
Defense40 %+ dig rate; chases every ball; excellent pattern recognition

Not sure where you fit?

Come to one of our open Rep or Clinic sessions — they're designed for exactly this. A coach will watch you play and advise the right level for your game. It's also the path to getting pre-approved for Advanced and Competitive sessions.
